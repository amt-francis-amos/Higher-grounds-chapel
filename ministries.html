OUR MINISTRIES

The Children's Ministry of Higher Grounds participates in District programs involving quizzes and sword drill. In most cases, the came 3rd. The Men's Ministry also took part in District & Regional programs. In 2012, we were involved in Evangelism in Asaake area and did very well in bringing in new converts to Asaake church. From 2009 to 2011, the Youth ministry of Higher Grounds was consistently 1st in quizzes organized by the District. The Church was rewarded as the Best in Sunday school organization. The Church was given a citation in 2009. We were also recognized as the church who attended Sunday school Seminar in our numbers.
